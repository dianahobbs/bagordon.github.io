How We Meet

Lab meetings are typically every Friday afternoon from 1-2 PM either in person or over Microsoft Teams, and individual meetings with Brian will be scheduled on a bi-weekly(?) basis. We use Teams for scheduling purposes and WUSTL Box for maintaining documents.

We use Box Word Documents to set agendas, record decisions made, and outline action items during meetings. Each lab member should have their own Word Doc for their 1:1 meetings with Brian. Prior to each meeting, create your agenda for that meeting there.


Beneficial Meetings

Monday 10-10:30 AM: Imaging Core "Processing" Meeting
1st Monday 10:30-11:00 AM: Benzinger Lab Meeting
Tuesday 12-1:00 PM: Knight ADRC or MIR Seminar
Wednesday 12-1:00 PM: Neuroimaging in Health and Disease Seminar
Thursday 11-11:30 AM: Publications and Projects Meeting
Friday 1-2:00 PM: BAGL Meeting
