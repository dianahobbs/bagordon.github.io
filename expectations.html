Lab Expectations


Code of Conduct

We strive to create an inclusive, collaborative, and respectful environment where all members can thrive. We embrace diverse perspectives and foster an atmosphere of open communication. We value integrity and uphold ethical standards for the responsible use of resources and data. Harassment, discrimination, and any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. We work together as a team, supporting and uplifting one another, and recognizing the contributions of all members. By adhering to this code of conduct, we create a positive and enriching experience for everyone in our lab and university community. How We Give Feedback

Most of the feedback we give and receive is when giving and attending practice talks. We expect feedback to be supportive, but constructive.

Individual feedback will be given on a 1, 5, and 12 month basis for new students and hires. After your first year, feedback is given on an annual basis.
