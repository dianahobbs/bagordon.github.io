Brian A. Gordon Lab (BAGL) Manual

Welcome to our lab!!

Welcome to our neuroimaging lab! This manual serves as a comprehensive resource, offering insights into our lab's operations and establishing clear expectations for our team members. It serves as a valuable repository of institutional knowledge, emcompassing essential information about procedures and the resources available to support our work. We encourage active participation from lab members and invite suggestions for additions or modifications to further enhance the manual's content. For any recommendations, please reach out to Brian (bagordon@wustl.edu), who will gladly address your inquiries.
