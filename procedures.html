The lab is committed to supporting the career development of its members. Brian will make every effort to secure grants and funding to support individuals for a couple of years. However, funding availability is competitive and unpredictable. Lab members are encouraged to actively pursue external funding opportunities, and the PI will provide guidance and mentorship throughout the application process. Taking an active role in seeking funding enhances skills and opens doors for future career opportunities. The lab values and supports the career growth of its members and strives to provide resources to advance research and professional aspirations.

Funding and Budgeting

For individuals at different stages of their academic career, the following are grant opportunities available to support Alzheimer's research.
It is important to note that grant availability, eligibility criteria, and application deadlines may vary. Researchers should carefully review the specific requirements of each grant and consider consulting with their institution's grant office or research support services for additional guidance.

  • Undergraduate Students

    • Alzheimer's Association Research Grant for Undergraduate Students
      BrightFocus Foundation Alzheimer's Disease Research Grant for Undergraduates

  • Graduate Students

    • National Institute on Aging (NIA) F31 Ruth L. Kirschstein National Research Service Award (NRSA) for Individual Predoctoral Fellows (Pre-doctoral)
      BrightFocus Foundation Alzheimer's Disease Research Grant for Graduate Students
      Tau Consortium Young Investigator Award for Alzheimer's Research

  • Postdoctoral Researchers

    • National Institute on Aging (NIA) F32 Ruth L. Kirschstein National Research Service Award (NRSA) for Individual Postdoctoral Fellows (Post-doctoral)
      BrightFocus Foundation Alzheimer's Disease Research Grant for Postdoctoral Fellows
      Alzheimer's Drug Discovery Foundation Postdoctoral Fellowship Program

  • Early Career Investigators

    • National Institute on Aging (NIA) K01 Mentored Research Scientist Development Award
      BrightFocus Foundation Alzheimer's Disease Research Grant for Early Career Investigators
      Alzheimer's Association Research Fellowship for Early Career Investigators

  • Established Researchers

    • National Institute on Aging (NIA) R01 Research Project Grant
      BrightFocus Foundation Alzheimer's Disease Research Grant for Established Investigators
      Alzheimer's Association Investigator-Initiated Research Grant

  • Collaborative Research

    • National Institute on Aging (NIA) R01 Research Project Grant (Multi-PI)
      Alzheimer's Association Part the Cloud Translational Research Funding
      Alzheimer's Drug Discovery Foundation Diagnostic Accelerator Grant
